About CodeFiesta

M-Pulse is an annual technical event of P.E.S’s Modern College of Engineering. CodeFiesta being the Computer Engineering Department division of M-Pulse.

Ever since M-Pulse was initiated in 2006, the technomanagement event of the college has grown in size, name and grandeur. We have worked hard over this period to raise the benchmark each year to deliver more than what was expected from us. Apart from taking a classy turn this year, we present you with a much more technically enriched event by introducing CodeFiesta 2020.

Four technical events/competitions will be organised under CodeFiesta where 350+ students are expected to participate from number of renowned Engineering colleges spread over Pune. Our agenda is to provide a platform where students/participants get opportunities to showcase their talent and technical skills.So join us as we take this giant leap into CodeFiesta 2019.

Tech Wizard

Participants will be tested on their skills on all broad domain of technology, from general knowledge about languages, to coding a program in a programming language.

ROUND 1
All MCQ’s will be based on programming languages. The time limit is of 15 minutes
Round 2
Blind Coding it is. As the name suggests, in this round participant will be coding for 10 minutes with monitor off after which the monitor will be turned on for a minutes following which participant will continue coding for next 5 minutes.
Registration fees
Individual: ₹ 50
Team of 2: ₹ 90

Pro Googler

Do you have a knack to Google every problem that you face everytime? Well, this event is the “ONE”. Event is merely based on the “art of googling” using various search patterns. This is one event which will really depict the power of the internet.

ROUND 1
Participants face a number of questions that need to be answered within a specified time limit. Participants that answer maximum correct questions within the time advance to the next round.
Round 2
The second round has more questions and less time allotted for it. The process remains the same but the answers are not obtained easily.
Registration fees
Per head: ₹ 50

Crack'it

Are you a fan of quizzes, trivia games? Are you the wizard who recognizes company names by mere sight of their logos? Do you think you are smart enough to know about the movies trending these days? Then my friend look no further, Crack’ it is made for you! Embark upon an adventure comprising of logo quiz as you advance to the section of favourite TV trivia.

ROUND 1
The participants will be facing 4 stages comprising 20 logo questions each. There is a predefined time limit to answer the questions asked. The top teams answering maximum questions within the “15 minutes” time frame advance to the next round.
Round 2
In this particular round, the participants will have to solve the MCQ’s on trending topics within specified time of 15 minutes.
Registration fees
Individual: ₹ 50
Team of 2: ₹ 90

Maze Coders

Its about time to combine fun as well as learning by bringing the classic Hangman game with technical keywords in one activity together! Are you the seasoned veteran that knows everything there is about the Linux File System? Give it a try!!!

ROUND 1
Participants play technical trivia based on Hangman game to find the hint and clear the first round.
Round 2
Clues in the form of files will be hidden in the Linux File System and you will have to traverse throught the files to get the final clue and solve the problem.
Registration fees
Individual: ₹ 50
Team of 2: ₹ 90

Runtime Rummage

Do adventures fancy you? Are you the Sherlock Holmes who likes to find the clues? Are you good at remembering patterns? Do participate in this event to test your abilities!

ROUND 1
This is a Treasure hunt round. The clues will be scattered all across the campus. Participants will have to find the clues based on their instincts to reach the final clue which ultimately leads towards the hidden treasure.
Round 2
This round is based on the game of Mahjong. There will be pages having codes written on it flipped upside down. Participant will have to find out matching codes that produce the same output within the time limit.
Registration fees
Team of 2: ₹ 90

Previous Year CodeFiesta

CodeFiesta is a technical event organized by the Computer Department of P.E.S. Modern College of Engineering, Shivaji Nagar, Pune. This event has been running for three years now. Each year students from the Computer Department try to outdo the previous year with better competitions, management & the entertainment factor.

CodeFiesta 2019

CodeFiesta 2019 was back with several technical events such as 'Tech wizard', 'Pro googler', 'Maze coders' and 'Crack it '. CodeFiesta is a completely student organised event. Every event tested diverse technical skills of students ranging from logical analysis, Linux directory handling, command line to actual coding in C , C++, Java and Python.
They needed to take a problem given on the spot to a solution in as less time as possible. Thus this events gave students an opportunity to showcase their technical and coding abilities. We believe in practical learning and aim to spread the art of technology among all the students. The footfall for 2019 codeFiesta was 450+. We believe that as a human we have some responsibilities towards the society and we aim to fulfill it through a social initiative. We had organised an event for orphan girls under codeFiesta 2019,called digital knowledge.

CodeFiesta 2018

CodeFiesta 2018 brought various tournaments for students from many different colleges in Pune. These included ‘Tech Wizard’, ‘Pro-Googler’, ‘Maze Coders’, ‘Crack it’. The tournaments were developed entirely from scratch by the students of the computer department. Every tournament tested diverse technical skills of the participants ranging from logical analysis, Linux directory handling, command line to actual coding in C, C++, Java & Python.
Participants incorporated their presence of mind and dexterity with their problem solving skills to achieve ranks in the tournaments. They needed to take a problem given on the spot to a solution in as less time as possible. Thus by reaping the benefits of competition they got a platform to boast their coding abilities. With upwards of 350 participants vigorously competing to prove their technical prowess CodeFiesta was very successful bringing a promise of a much bigger and better event in the forthcoming years.
